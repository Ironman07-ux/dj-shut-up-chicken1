The Short Version

Ian and Greyson were two neighborhood kids who grew up on mixtapes, late-night radio, and vinyl. They dreamed of club lights and subwoofers. One fateful night… they woke up with feathers.

A Sad Backstory — Full Version

Music was their escape from hardship. When the Colonel’s cursed gig turned them into chickens, they didn’t stop. They found a way to turn sorrow into rhythm, and From Coop to Club became their truth.

This site tells the story of Ian & Greyson — music, feathers, and disco redemption.
